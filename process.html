Reflection

I feel like this semester I’ve worked less as a constant unified design process week after week and more of design sprints. Smaller moments of intense work that move the projects ahead a lot. I think for next time I should work more in a process and leave more time for interaction and allowing ideas to form for naturally and in a less stressed environment. Though there were definitely some unforeseen stuff that came up over this sem, so maybe my critique is a little undue. I’ve deftly grown as a designer, and I think I’m a lot of intentional with my sketching and wireframing as well as built on my web design abilities. Also, this whole journal is good, when there issue much work to do, I tent to have a bit of a tunnel vision for the next, and this definitely helps to expand that just a little bit.
