Quote from class by Steve Jobs

“Most people make the mistake of thinking design is what it looks like. That’s not what we think design is. It’s not just what it looks like and feels like. Design is how it works.”

In class

In the tutorial we did a lot of different sketching exercises to unpack and to kinda redefine how we think about sketching, that it’s a tool for thinking, communicating and persuading. The last part of the three was the one that I thought of being the most interesting. I’d never thought of it being a persuasive method. But it makes sense, it’s a tool to encode meaning and value. I thought that was a really helpful way of thinking.
